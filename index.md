## Bio
I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Maryland, 
advised by <a href="https://www.cs.umd.edu/~sfeizi/">Prof. Feizi</a> and <a href="https://www.cs.umd.edu/~hajiagha/">Prof. Hajiaghayi</a>.  My research focuses on the interpretability of generative AI models from both a model perspective‚Äîlocalizing knowledge within models, detecting, and explaining their failure modes‚Äîand a data perspective, analyzing the impact of individual data points on a model through challenges like unlearning and data selection for language model *pretraining*.
Furthermore, I have proposed methods for integrating ads into the output of LLMs as a strategy to monetize them effectively.
